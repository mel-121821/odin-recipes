Creamy Cauliflower Chower

The perfect winter comfort food!

This is one of my favourite soup recipes. Best served with toasted french bread, It's quick, easy and delicious, even for picky eaters!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head of cauliflower, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp of dried thyme
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour, can use gluten-free flour
  • 4 cups of broth, chicken, beef or veg will all work for this
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese, I use old cheddar
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Toss Cauliflower and whole garlic cloves in the olive oil with some salt and pepper. Place on a baking sheet and roast for 20 minutes, turning once.
  2. In a large pot, melt butter over medium-high heat, add onion and cook for 2-3 minutes, then add carrots and celery and cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occationally.
  3. Finely chop the roasted galic cloves. Add the garlic, roasted cauliflower, bay leaf and dried thyme to the pot. Sprinkle flour over the vegetables and stir. Cook until flour disappears.
  4. Pour in broth, stir and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in the milk and shredded cheese until cheese is melted and chowder is creamy. Season with salt and pepper is desired.
  5. Ladle chowder into bowls and serve warm.
  6. For an extra touch, slice some french bread 1 1/4 inch thick, brush with melted butter and broil for 2 minutes, turning once
  7. Enjoy!
