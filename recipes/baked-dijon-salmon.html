Baked Dijon Salmon

Savoury, sweet and delicious, you'll be eating this right out of the pan!

Salmon has always been one of my favourite foods growing up. It was one of those things where I would always go back for seconds, no matter how full I was. In the past, I'd always had it with lemon and dill, but a few years ago I found this recipe and salmon dinner took on a whole new meaning! Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 4 4oz salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup of butter,melted
  • 3 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 1/4 cup of bread crumbs
  • a dash of dried parsley

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together butter, mustard and honey
  3. Brush the salmon with the mixture and sprinkle with breadcrumbs and parsley.
  4. Bake for 25 minuites.
  5. You can also broil for an extra 2-4 minutes to get a nice browinig on the breadcrumbs
