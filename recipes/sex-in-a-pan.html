Sex in a Pan

Layers of chocolatey goodness!

The name says it all folks! One of the best desserts ever, and no baking involved! With 4 delicious layers of graham crust, chocolate pudding, cream cheese and whipped cream, it's super easy to make. Just try not to eat it all in one sitting!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 8oz block of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 envelope of whipped cream or 2 cups of cool whip, divided
  • one pakage of instant pudding. Chocolate is recommended for this version, but feel free to use any flavour you wish.
  • one large chocolate bar, shaved
  • Make sure you check the graham crumb, pudding and whipped cream packages to ensure you have the ingredients you need to follow their instructions.

Steps:

  1. Before assembling the layers, prepare the instant pudding as described on the box. Place in the fridge to thicken.
  2. Also prepare the whipped cream if using the envelope, and set aside.
  3. For first layer, prepare the graham cracker crust as directed on the box. Layer it on the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan and set aside.
  4. For second layer, whip the cream cheese and gradually add sugar. Beat until smooth, add whipped cream (either 1/2 the envelope or 1 cup of cool whip) and mix again. Place this layer on top of the graham crust.
  5. Check the pudding to see if it's thickened. If it has, spread it over the cream cheese layer.
  6. Spread the remaining whipped cream or cool whip on top of the pudding layer.
  7. Sprinkle chocolate shavings on top.
  8. Enjoy!
